Mushroom Salsa Chili

Presented by Sarah Hoppe for her DTC 477 Course in Fall 2022!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk pork sausage
  • 2 cans, 16oz each, kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 3 cups chunky salsa
  • 1 can, 14.5oz, diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 can, 8oz, tomato sauce
  • 1 can, 4oz, mushroom stems and pieces, drained
  • 1/2 cup each copped green, sweet red, and yellow peppers
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/8 teaspoon dried marjoram
  • Additional Toppings:
    • Shredded sharp cheddar cheese
    • Sour cream
    • Thinly sliced green onions

Kitchen Equipment

  • Large skillet
  • Slow cooker
  • Spatula
  • Measuring cups
  • Can opener
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, cook beef and sausage over medium heat 6-8 minutes or until no longer pink, breaking into crumbles.
  2. Drain all the meat and transfer to a 5- or 6-quart slow cooker.
  3. Stir in beans, salsa, tomatoes, onion, tomato sauce, mushrooms, peppers and seasonings.
  4. Cook, covered, on low 8-10 hours or until flavors are blended.
  5. Add toppings of your choice and serve!
